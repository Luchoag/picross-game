How to Play

In this game, also known as Nonogram or Hanjie, your goal is to find the shape hidden in the board. To do so, you will have to fill the correct cells of the grid with black squares. The numbers surrounding the grid indicate how many continuous black squares are in that row or column. If there is more than one number in a row, it means you will first find the sequence of the first number and then the sequence of the other/s number/s, separated from each other by at least one blank square. The cells where there are no black squares can remain unfilled, but it's recommended to fill them with a gray square (click/tap twice) to help you solve the puzzle. When you think you're done filling the grid, click the "Check Puzzle" button to see if you did it right.
